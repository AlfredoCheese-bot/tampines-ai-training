Module 01 · The Booth Routine

The 5-step sales flow

Visitors at Tampines Hub are families, seniors and students browsing 30+ showcases. You have 2–5 minutes per person. Run this loop every time.

01

Greet

Open with a hook, never "Can I help you?"

"Want to see a gadget that writes your meeting notes for you?"
02

Discover

One question decides which product to show.

"Is this for work, study, or someone at home?"
03

Demo

Show, don't tell. 10 seconds of speech → live transcript. Put the device in their hands.

04

Pitch

30 seconds: one benefit, one proof point, one price anchor.

05

Close

Point to the QR standee.

"Scan here — it opens iShopChangi, and you save the GST. Checkout takes under a minute."
💰 The GST-savings close: buying via the QR on iShopChangi means GST savings versus regular retail — often the difference of a nice dinner. Use it on every fence-sitter: "Same product, official channel, delivered to your home — minus the GST. The QR price is lower than any store you'll walk into." If they hesitate: "Scan it anyway — the page stays in your browser so you can decide tonight."

Demo rescue — when something glitches mid-pitch

  • The 30-second rule: never troubleshoot in front of a customer for more than 30 seconds. Switch to the backup unit, or play the product video — every product card above links one.
  • Keep one "golden unit" per product charged, logged in and untouched; rotate it in when a demo unit misbehaves.
  • Wi-Fi drops → hotspot from the opening checklist. QR won't scan → search the product together on ishopchangi.com.
  • Log every glitch and report nightly to the supervisor — tomorrow's setup fixes today's surprises.

Module 08 · Booth Conduct & Run-of-Show

Do's, Don'ts & logistics

Do

  • Keep all 6 demo units charged; QR standee visible and tested every morning
  • Demo in the visitor's preferred language where supported
  • End every conversation with the QR ask — even browsers
  • Hand devices to customers; touch sells
  • Be honest about limits (e.g. Liberty case records in-person audio only)
  • Tell people you're recording before demo recordings
  • Log unanswered questions; report nightly to the supervisor

Don't

  • Don't quote prices from memory — open the iShopChangi listing live; prices there carry the GST savings
  • Don't invent specs. "Let me check that for you" beats a wrong answer
  • Don't process any payment at the booth — all checkout is QR → iShopChangi
  • Don't crowd one visitor while others wait — close or hand off
  • Don't leave the booth unmanned; coordinate breaks
  • Don't bad-mouth other exhibitors' products — compare on facts only

Where & when

Our Tampines Hub, 1 Tampines Walk — Metapod booth, Interactive Experience zone. 19–21 June, 12pm–8pm. (Static displays ran 16–18 Jun; our window is the interactive days.) Arrive 30 min early for setup.

Opening checklist

  • 6 demo units charged, apps logged in
  • Test-scan the QR yourself
  • Wi-Fi / hotspot live for demos
  • Brochures stocked, counter tidy

Checkout path

Visitor scans QR → iShopChangi product page → add to cart → pays on their phone with GST savings, home delivery. QR fails? Search the product name on ishopchangi.com together.

Crowd notes

Expect families, seniors, students — often together. Weekend (20–21 Jun) peaks after 2pm. Keep pitches short during peaks; the exhibition also runs workshops and talks that release crowds in waves.

Closing checklist

  • Charge all units overnight
  • Secure demo devices and stock
  • Report the day's FAQs and objections

Escalate to supervisor

  • Warranty / refund questions
  • Bulk or corporate purchase enquiries
  • Press, influencer or organiser requests
  • Faulty demo units
